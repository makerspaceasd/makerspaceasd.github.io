Safety and Policies

Make Safe.


At the core of the Makerspace is student, faculty, and staff safety. Make Safe ensures activities are conducted in the safest way possible for oneself and others. The Falcon Way supports a culture of positive, trusting relationships between all stakeholders and a happy, high-functioning community. It includes clear expectations of student and teacher behavior that reflect our Core Values, and both students and teachers must consistently meet these expectations in the Makerspace.
